The Manulife Dragon Boat Festival is an organized Fundraiser in support of the Nova Scotia Amateur Sport Fund. Teams of 20 people, plus a drummer, paddle Dragon Boats down the lake in a series of fun and friendly races. Dragon boats are very stable, and the races only last a minute or two, making it a perfect event for everyone - even a team of beginners!

This year's event will take place on Saturday, July 11, 2009 on Lake Banook in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, from 9:00a.m.-4:00p.m. We look forward to seeing you there!
